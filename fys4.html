IATF 16949


This is the global technical specification and quality management standard for the automotive industry. The ISO/TS 16949 was originally created in 1999 to harmonize different assessment and certification schemes worldwide in the supply chain for the automotive sector.


Background


International Automotive Task Force (IATF) is a group of automotive manufacturers ( which includes The BMW Group, Daimler AG, Ford Motor Company, Renault, Volkswagen AG and Vehicle Manufacturersâ€™ Trade Associations among others), who aim at providing improved quality products to automotive customers worldwide. The IATF 16949 is a QMS prepared by IATF and the Technical Committee of ISO to provide for continual improvement, emphasizing on defect prevention, variation reduction and wastage in the supply chain of the automotive industry.


What is IATF?


IATF 16949:2016 outlines everything you need to know about achieving best practice when designing, developing, manufacturing, installing or servicing automotive products.


This standard, combined with applicable Customer-Specific Requirements (CSRâ€™s), defines the QMS requirements for automotive production, service and/or accessory parts.

  • Promotes the idea of continual improvement and Emphasizes defect prevention.


  • Promotes reduction of variation and waste in the supply chain.


  • Facilitates documented and implemented corporate responsibility polices.

    • Who Needs this Standard?


    It is applicable to any supplier providing design and development, production companies, and, when relevant, assembly, installation and services of automotive related products, including products with embedded software.


    The IATF 16949 standard is also applicable to sites of the organization where manufacturing of customer-specified production parts, service parts, and/or accessory parts occur.


    When to Implement IATF 16949


    Compliance to the IATF 16949 standard can be done at any time but is typically used when:


  • Customers specify it as a part of their contract.


  • Organizations want to enhance their product delivery and customer satisfaction
