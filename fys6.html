ISO 39001


Road Traffic Safety (RTS) Management System


What is ISO 39001?


ISO 39001:2012 specifies requirements for a road traffic safety (RTS) management system for organizations to reduce death and serious injuries related to road traffic crashes. This includes development and implementation of an appropriate RTS policy, objectives and action plans based on what can be controlled or influenced. The system provides an adaptable high level framework and shares a structure with other international management system standards, such as ISO 9001, ISO 45001, and ISO 27001.
