ISO 29990


ISO 29990 is a quality management system standard for providers of education and training services. This Standard provides a generic model for quality professional practice and performance for learning service providers (LSPs) in the conceptualization, development and delivery of education and training.


This Certification provides you with a transparent analysis of your present program and a valuable tool for developing effective, learner-centered training. This Standard can be integrated with many other management system standards.


Who Needs ISO 29990?


This Standard applies to education providers in the non-formal education (organized form of education aimed at a specific target audience) and is particularly suitable for educational institutes and academies, in-house training facilities, trade schools, vocational schools, colleges and universities.


Advantage ISO 29990


  • Enhances global marketability of education and training products.


  • Enhanced delivery quality.


  • Transparency and comparability across delivery levels.


  • Provision of a common global reference for customers in the design, development and implementation of learning.


  • Opportunities for educational collaborations and development in the area of education and training.


  • Global benchmarking for quality and excellence.


  • Increased visibility and public trust.


  • Unified delivery standards.


  • Reduced costs and increased customer satisfaction.


  • Sales and marketing advantages.


  • Greater control over processes and internal systems.
