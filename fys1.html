ISO 9001:2015


ISO 9001 is a globally recognized International Standard that specifies requirements for a Quality Management System (QMS).


Most popular in the ISO 9000 series, it is the only Standard to which organizations need to certify.


The current version of ISO 9001 was released in 2015.


Over one million organizations across the world use this certification making it the most acceptable worldwide standard for Quality. It is also a good foundation for implementing many other management standards.


    Who Needs ISO 9001:2015?


    It is a Company certification not a product certification.

  • It applies to any organization, regardless of size or industry.

  • Organizations using ISO 9001 can migrate to ISO 9001:2015.

    • Areas Covered


    ISO 9001 provides a process-oriented methodology based on a PLAN- DO-CHECK-ACT mechanism that extends to documentation and review of structure, responsibilities, and procedures required to achieve effective quality management in an organization.


    The following areas are covered under this standard:


  • Requisites for a QMS which includes documentation, planning and identification of process interactions


  • Management responsibilities


  • Management of information, resources, human resources and work eco-systems


  • Product or Service realization from design to delivery


  • Measurement, analysis, and improvement of the QMS through activities


    • How Long Does it Take to get this Certification?


    This depends on:


  • Organizational needs and requirements


  • The availability of resources


  • Work scope


  • Complexities in execution
